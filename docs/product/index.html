This webpage is a TikTok-Vinted-Tinder morph.

As a registered user, you can instantly buy items or skip them for fast dopamine delivery.

You can also sell your own items for others to buy.

To see your spending streak, a transaction history is available.

Whenever you want, you can change your user profile to your likings.
